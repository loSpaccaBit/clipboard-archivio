FAQ

Before you install

What are the requirements?

macOS 26 or later, Apple Silicon Mac (arm64). The app lives in the menu bar and does not need the Dock.

macOS says the app is unverified — what do I do?

Right-click Appunti Archivio → Open, then confirm. This is only needed on first launch because the app is not signed with an Apple Developer certificate.

Are my clips sent online?

No. Everything stays on your Mac. The encrypted vault uses local AES-GCM; sensitive data detection runs on-device.

Can I view or modify the code?

Yes. The project is open source under GNU GPL v3.0+ on GitHub.
